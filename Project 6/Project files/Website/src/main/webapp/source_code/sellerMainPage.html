Website Information

This is the flea market website where SungKyunKwan University students can freely buy and sell books. Any student at SungKyunKwan University can use this website.

For Sellers,
- Take out the unread books from your bookshelf and put them on the market.
- Pass on the major books that you don't use anymore to your juniors.
- Books priced at reasonable prices will be popular.
- Buyers will look around the site and look at the books they are interested in.

For Buyers,
- Buy the book you wanted to read at reasonable price.
- Find the major books need for your class.
- The popular book will be sold soon, so hurry up.
- Sellers will offer various books for you at reasonable price.

Please feel free to enjoy the service provided by the website.
